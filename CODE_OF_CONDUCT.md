# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want Kronos to be a respectful, inclusive, and technically healthy project for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in feedback and discussion.
- Critique code, not people.
- Share context and be patient with learners.
- Assume good intent while reviewing contributions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Intimidation or sustained disruption of discussion

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this policy.

## Reporting conduct issues

Report incidents through private channels available on the repository profile.
